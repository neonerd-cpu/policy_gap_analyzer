INCIDENT RESPONSE POLICY

Version: 1.0
Date: January 1, 2026

1. PURPOSE

To establish procedures for responding to security incidents.

2. SCOPE

All employees and systems.

3. POLICY

3.1 Incident Identification

Security incidents include:
- Unauthorized access
- Malware infections
- Data breaches
- System outages

3.2 Reporting

All incidents must be reported immediately to the IT helpdesk.

3.3 Response Team

The Incident Response Team includes:
- IT Manager
- Security Officer
- Legal Counsel

3.4 Containment

Upon detection, affected systems should be isolated.

3.5 Documentation

All incidents must be logged with:
- Date and time
- Type of incident
- Actions taken
- Resolution

3.6 Review

Incidents are reviewed monthly by management.
