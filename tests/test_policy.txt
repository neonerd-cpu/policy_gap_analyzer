Information Security Policy

1. Purpose
This policy defines the organization's approach to information security.

2. Access Control
Only authorized personnel may access critical systems. Passwords must be strong.

3. Data Protection
Sensitive data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Incident Response
All security incidents must be reported to the IT security team within 24 hours.

5. Backup and Recovery
Critical data must be backed up daily and recovery procedures tested quarterly.

6. Risk Management
Risks must be identified, assessed, and mitigated according to risk appetite.

End of Policy.
