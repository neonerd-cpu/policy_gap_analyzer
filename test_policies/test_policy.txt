INFORMATION SECURITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (ISMS) POLICY

1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to define the organizationâ€™s commitment to protecting
information assets from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees and systems within the organization.

3. Information Security Objectives
The organization shall implement security controls to ensure confidentiality, integrity,
and availability of information.

4. Roles and Responsibilities
Management is responsible for ensuring that information security controls are implemented.
Employees shall comply with security requirements.

5. Access Control
Access to systems shall be provided based on business requirements.
User credentials shall be kept confidential.

6. Data Protection
Sensitive information shall be protected against unauthorized disclosure.
Data shall be stored securely.

7. Patch Management
Systems shall be updated periodically to address security vulnerabilities.

8. Risk Management
Security risks shall be identified and addressed as necessary.

9. Policy Review
This policy shall be reviewed periodically.
